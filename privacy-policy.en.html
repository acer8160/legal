Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Ur Metronome (the “App”) handles user information. The developer and operator of the App (“we”) respects users’ privacy and follows a basic policy of collecting only the minimum information necessary.

1. Basic Policy

The App does not collect personally identifiable information (personal information) such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise location. We only collect anonymous usage data and the purchase information required to process billing.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Anonymous Usage Data (Analytics)

To improve the App, we collect anonymous usage data such as the following:

  • Screen views and transitions, and feature usage (e.g., metronome start, preset save, paywall display)
  • Technical information such as the app version, OS type, and general device type

This data is collected under an anonymous identifier that cannot identify you, and is not linked to personal information such as your name. You can turn this collection off at any time from the settings screen (Section 5).

2.2 Purchase and Subscription Information

To process and manage the purchase, restoration, and status of the Pro subscription, we handle purchase transaction information (such as whether a purchase was made and the subscription expiration date) through Apple and our billing infrastructure provider. Payment information such as credit card numbers is processed by Apple, and we do not receive it.

2.3 Information Stored Only on Your Device

Practice records (BPM history, goals, presets, notes, etc.) are stored only in the database on your device and are never transmitted to our servers.

3. Purposes of Use

We use the collected information only for the following purposes:

  • Providing the App’s features, improving quality and convenience, and identifying issues
  • Improving the user experience through trend analysis such as drop-off and retention
  • Providing and managing the Pro subscription (purchase, renewal, restoration)

4. Provision to Third Parties and Service Providers

We do not sell or provide collected information to third parties without the user’s consent. However, to the extent necessary to operate the App, we use the following service providers:

ProviderPurposeInformation handled
Apple Inc.App distribution and payment processingPurchase and payment information

Please refer to each provider’s official website for their respective privacy policies.

5. Opting Out of Usage Data Collection

You can stop the collection of usage data (Section 2.1) at any time by turning off “Send usage data” in the App’s settings screen. After stopping, no new usage data will be sent.

6. Data Storage Location

Anonymous usage data is stored on servers located within the EU (European Union).

7. Children’s Privacy

The App is not primarily directed at any particular age group and does not collect personal information.

8. Security

We and our service providers take reasonable security measures to prevent the leakage, loss, or alteration of the information we handle.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may revise this Policy as necessary. If there are significant changes, we will update the effective date on this page to notify you.
